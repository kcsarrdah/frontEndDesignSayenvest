About Us

SayeNvest Capital is a global boutique advisory firm. We focus on strategic, financial and family office advisory services as well as fund and capital raising through our global network of institutional investors and family offices. Our expertise lies in Automotive, Banking and Financial Services, Digital Assets and currencies, Emerging technologies, Energy including renewables, Entertainment, Information technology including enabled services; Infrastructure, Media, Mining, Oil and Gas, Real Estate and Telecom. Our team takes pride in the agility for our global reach and our expansive network which we leverage to access capital, expertise and bridge unique business opportunities to our clients.
